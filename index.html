What I do

The work

1) Paid file review

I look at your QuickBooks and statements to confirm scope and price the cleanup as a flat project.

  • Review reconciliation status and gaps.
  • Identify missing months, duplicates, and uncategorized activity.
  • Flat price + timeline for cleanup.

2) Cleanup & catch-up

I bring your books current and tie out the accounts to the penny.

  • Reconcile bank and credit card accounts in scope.
  • Clean up categorization so the P&L is usable.
  • Fix common issues (duplicates, opening balance problems, mis-postings).

3) Monthly close (optional)

After cleanup, I keep your books current so you can pull clean numbers anytime.

  • Monthly reconciliations.
  • P&L + balance sheet on a set date.
  • Clean financials for a loan request, partner, or your tax pro at year-end.

What you get

Simple deliverables that match what people actually ask for.

  • Clean reconciliations (no mystery balances).
  • Reports that make sense (month, quarter, YTD).
  • A clear handoff to your tax preparer at year-end.

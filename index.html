The work

1) Paid diagnostic (required)

I look at your QuickBooks and statements to confirm scope, find what’s broken, and price the cleanup as a flat project.

  • Review your QuickBooks file and reconciliation status.
  • Identify missing months, duplicate activity, and uncategorized items.
  • Give you a flat cleanup price and a timeline.

2) Cleanup & catch-up

I bring your books current and tie out the accounts to the penny.

  • Reconcile all bank and credit card accounts in scope.
  • Clean up categorization so your P&L is usable.
  • Fix common messes (duplicates, opening balance issues, mis-postings).

3) Monthly close (optional)

If you want to stay current after cleanup, I can handle a simple monthly close.

  • Monthly reconciliations.
  • Monthly P&L + balance sheet on a set date.
  • Short list of issues that need your attention.

How it works

  • 1) Diagnostic: you share access + statements; I confirm scope and price it.
  • 2) Cleanup: I reconcile, clean up history, and fix the structure.
  • 3) Handover: you get clean reports and a clear next step.
